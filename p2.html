Abstract

We are witnessing the dawn of a new era of the Internet of Things (IOT; also known as the Internet of Objects). Generally, speaking IOT refers to the networked interconnection of everyday objects, which are often equipped with ubiquitous intelligence. IOT will increase the ubiquity of the Internet by integrating every object for interaction via embedded systems, which leads to a highly distributed network of devices communicating with human beings as well as other devices.

There are many applications of IOT in our daily lives, which will be discussed in that research. Besides, I want to talk and discuss more about the obstacles facing the scientists in that field.
